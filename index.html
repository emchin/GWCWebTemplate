About Me

My name is Emily and I'm a coder! I've been coding for the last two years, and I'm having an amazing time with amazing people. Currently, I am a freshman at UCSD; I'm studying Cognitive Science with a focus in ML and AI. When I'm not looking through my code, I enjoy reading murder mysteries, playing with my Shih Tzu, learning new languages, and crocheting.
