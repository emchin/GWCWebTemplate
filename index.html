About Me

My name is Emily and I'm a coder! I've been coding for the last six months, and I'm having an amazing time with amazing people. Currently, I am a part of Women in Tech, Girls Who Code, and Tinovation. I've attended Firebird Hacks and PixelHacks II, and I look forward to attending more hackathons in my future! When I'm not looking through my code, I enjoy reading Norweigan murder mysteries, playing with my Shih Tzu, learning new languages, and crocheting.
